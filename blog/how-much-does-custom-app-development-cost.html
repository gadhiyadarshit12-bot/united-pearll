How Much Does Custom App Development Cost in 2026? Pricing Guide

📅 July 8, 2026 ⏱ 6 min read ✍️ United Pearl

For startups, businesses, and enterprise platforms ready to expand their digital reach, a mobile application is the ultimate tool. But one of the most significant variables in budgeting is understanding: "How much does it cost to build a mobile app?" The pricing fluctuates based on features, designs, integrations, and platforms. In this complete guide, we break down custom app development costs in 2026.

1. App Costs by Complexity & Type

Not all apps are built equal. A simple utility app will naturally cost less than a real-time social networking or fintech application. Here's a quick estimation of costs based on complexity:

  • Simple App (Single function, standard UI): $1,500 – $5,000. Examples: calculators, basic catalogs, PDF viewers.
  • Medium Complexity App (Dynamic UI, database integration, payments): $5,000 – $15,000. Examples: e-commerce stores, custom scheduling, customer portals.
  • High Complexity App (Real-time tracking, chat, AI logic, custom APIs): $15,000 – $40,000+. Examples: food delivery, SaaS products, advanced tracking networks.

2. How Development Platform Influences Cost

The choice between native and cross-platform coding is a primary budget driver. If you build natively for both Android (Kotlin) and iOS (Swift) separately, you are hiring two development teams, which doubles the cost. By choosing cross-platform frameworks like Flutter or React Native, you build a single app codebase that runs flawlessly on both systems. This saves up to 40% of the cost and significantly reduces the timeline.

3. Hidden & Ongoing Costs to Budget For

Building the app is just the first step. To keep it live, budget for these yearly requirements:

Service Estimated Annual Cost Frequency
App Store Developer Account $99 (Apple) / $25 (Google - one-time) Annual / One-time
Cloud Hosting & Server APIs $120 – $1,200 (scales with users) Monthly billing
Maintenance & OS Updates 10% to 20% of original development cost Ongoing support
